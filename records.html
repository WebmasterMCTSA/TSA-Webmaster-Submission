Court Records Access

Learn how to access, request, and review public court records in McCreary County.

Accessing Court Records

The McCreary County Fiscal Court maintains records in accordance with state law. Records may include meeting minutes, ordinances, resolutions, and other official documents.

How to Request Records

  • Submit a written request via mail or email to the Fiscal Court office
  • Provide the specific record name, date, or topic for accurate processing
  • Some records may require a nominal copying fee
