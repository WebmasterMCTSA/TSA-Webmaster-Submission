Upcoming Events & Meetings

Stay informed about Fiscal Court meetings, community programs, and local activities happening across McCreary County.

Government & Civic Engagement
  • Fiscal Court Meeting – Second Thursday of every month at 6:00 PM, Fiscal Courtroom, Whitley City.
  • Public Hearings – Budget, zoning, and ordinance discussions announced as needed.
  • Town Hall Meetings – Informal Q&A sessions with the Judge/Executive and Magistrates.
  • Budget Workshops – Learn how county funds are allocated to local programs.
  • Election Information Events – Voter registration and election education sessions.
Community & Public Services
  • Community Cleanup Day – May 15, 2025, 9:00 AM–3:00 PM, Various County Locations.
  • Recycling Drop-Off Day – First Saturday monthly, County Transfer Station.
  • Public Safety Awareness Week – Meet EMS, Fire, and Sheriff's Departments.
  • Volunteer Recognition Event – Honoring community volunteers and organizations.
  • Veterans Appreciation Ceremony – November 11, 2025, Courthouse Lawn.
Recreation & Tourism
  • McCreary County Fair – August 10–14, 2025, McCreary County Fairgrounds.
  • Historical Walking Tour – September 5, 2025, 10:00 AM, Downtown Whitley City.
  • Fall Heritage Festival – October 12, 2025, Sandhill Park.
  • Christmas Parade & Tree Lighting – December 6, 2025, Courthouse Square.
  • Concert in the Park Series – Summer evenings featuring local artists and food vendors.
Economic & Development
  • Small Business Roundtable – Quarterly networking for local entrepreneurs.
  • Grant & Funding Workshop – Learn about available county and state assistance programs.
  • Job & Resource Fair – June 20, 2025, McCreary Central High School Gym.
  • Infrastructure Project Updates – Open house discussions for upcoming county improvements.
Education & Transparency
  • Civic Education Series – "How County Government Works" sessions for the public.
  • Open Data Portal Launch – Learn how to access county data and resources.
  • School Partnership Events – Student shadow programs and government day activities.
