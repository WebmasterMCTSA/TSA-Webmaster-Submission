About Us

Nestled between the Daniel Boone National Forest and the Big South Fork NRRA, McCreary County is rich in natural beauty, outdoor adventure, and cultural heritage. Our elected officials work collaboratively to meet the needs of residents and provide effective governance.

  • Fiscal Court meets the second Thursday of every month to set policies, approve budgets, and oversee county claims.
  • Officials collaborate to maintain transparency and meaningful community engagement.
  • Responsible budgeting ensures essential public services are fully supported.
  • Public records are accessible under the Open Records Act for full transparency.

Court Records

The McCreary County Fiscal Court maintains records in accordance with state law, including meeting minutes, ordinances, resolutions, and official documents.

How to Request Records

  • Submit a written request via mail or email to the Fiscal Court office
  • Provide the specific record name, date, or topic for accurate processing
  • Some records may require a nominal copying fee
