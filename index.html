Welcome to McCreary County

About Us

Nestled between the Daniel Boone National Forest and the Big South Fork NRRA, McCreary County is rich in natural beauty, outdoor adventure, and cultural heritage. Explore landmarks like Natural Arch, historic Stearns, Barthell Coal Mining Camp, and the Sheltowee Trace Trail.

Quick Facts

Founded

Established in 1912 — Kentucky's youngest county.

Population

Approximately 16,887 residents (2024).

County Seat

Whitley City, Kentucky.

Scenic Beauty

Home to Daniel Boone National Forest and Big South Fork River.

Community Focus

Emphasizing service, integrity, and collaboration with residents.

Heritage

Rich Appalachian culture and outdoor recreation opportunities.
